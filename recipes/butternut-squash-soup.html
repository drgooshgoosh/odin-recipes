Butternut Squash Soup

Description

This creamy risotto is infused with the rich, nutty flavor of roasted butternut squash and aromatic sage. The creamy texture and slight sweetness of the squash balance beautifully with the savory Parmesan and buttery rice. Perfect for a cozy evening during the fall season.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 2 cups butternut squash, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Toss the butternut squash with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  2. In a pot, heat butter and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add Arborio rice and cook, stirring constantly, for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gradually add warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed before adding more.
  5. Once the rice is cooked and creamy, stir in the roasted squash, Parmesan cheese, and fresh sage.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.
